Disciplinary Committee (DC)

Roles & Responsibilities

The Disciplinary Committee (DC) is officially constituted to oversee matters related to discipline and the code of conduct within the association.

  • Maintain Decorum: Ensure the maintenance of discipline, decorum, and a positive academic environment across the campus.
  • Ensure Compliance: Monitor and enforce strict adherence to institutional guidelines and BMSA rules by all students.
  • Address Misconduct: Investigate complaints, address misconduct impartially, and recommend appropriate actions to maintain a fair and safe environment.
