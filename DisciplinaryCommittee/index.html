An independent body dedicated to ensuring ethical and professional conduct for all members of GMC Bundi.

Independent Body Fair & Confidential

Purpose & Scope

The Disciplinary Committee (DC) is responsible for maintaining the integrity of the BMSA constitution and ensuring organizational standards.

  • Responsibility: Ensuring the ethical, moral, and professional conduct of Executive Committee members and general BMSA members.

  • Authority: Investigating complaints, issuing warnings, and recommending suspension or removal upon finding violations.

Committee Composition

Advisor Members

Five (5) Advisor Members

Selection Criteria: Members are selected based on integrity, neutrality, and proven seniority within the association.
