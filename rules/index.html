⚖️ Disciplinary Removal Process

1

First Warning: Official written notice issued to the concerned member.

2

Formal Inquiry: Conducted if misconduct continues or is severe.

3

Final Decision: DC recommends suspension/removal to Executive Committee.

🚨 DC Special Powers

Emergency Suspension: DC may impose temporary suspension if a member poses an immediate threat to BMSA integrity.

Electoral Oversight: DC supervises election processes to prevent malpractice.

Crisis Intervention: DC intervenes in leadership disputes or organizational crises.
