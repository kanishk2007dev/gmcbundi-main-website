Welcome to GMC Bundi

" Government Medical College, Bundi is a premier institution established with the vision of bridging the gap in healthcare services and medical education. Affiliated with the Rajasthan University of Health Sciences and approved by the NMC, we are committed to academic excellence and holistic student development.

Our campus is equipped with modern infrastructure, advanced laboratories, and a teaching hospital that serves the community. We strive to produce competent medical professionals who are ethical, compassionate, and skilled in serving humanity.
